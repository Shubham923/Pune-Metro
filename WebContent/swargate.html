Swargate

Line 2 will run from Ramwadi to Vanaz via Mangalwar Peth and Deccan Gymkhana. The elevated line will cover a distance of 14.66 km and will have 16 stations. It will connect with Lines 1 and 3 at the Civil Court interchange station. The maintenance depot for Line 2 will be located near the Vanaz station on the former garbage depot land.
